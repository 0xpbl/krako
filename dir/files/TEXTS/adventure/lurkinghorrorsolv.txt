                         The Solution To:
                    L U R K I N G   H O R R O R
                     Written By:  The Sorcerer

   Welcome  to the Solution for Lurking  Horror!  Infocom's  first 
interactive horror adventure.. This adventure is pretty tricky and 
some of the puzzles have no room for error,  so follow those parts 
exactly!

    You've  just  fought your way over  to  the  computer  center, 
through   the  blizzard  that  has  been  blocking  off   all   of 
civilization  from  your colledge,  and  you're  espically  jumpy, 
because of the recent disappearences..

  First off,  SIT IN CHAIR and TURN PC ON.  If you bought the game 
(hah!)  look  on your ID card that came  with  it,  if  not,  TYPE 
872325412 and then TYPE UHLERSOTH then,  when the screen comes  on 
type  CLICK ON BOX and then G (again) Now that this  strange  text 
has appeared,  READ TEXT and then PUSH MORE.  When the next screen 
appears,  READ TEXT and then,  again,  PUSH MORE.  Now, quickly to 
avoid that strange picture, READ TEXT.

  What is this?  Where am I now?  Well, go DOWN and then GO TO THE 
PLATFORM.   The  crowd has gathered around you,  so GET STONE  and 
then wait, because you can't do anything..

  What?  Back in the Computer Lab?  But how did I get this  stone? 
The  hacker  will now come over and begin trying to sort  out  the 
mess  that happened...just wait until he is finished,  with the  Z 
command (wait)..After he returns to his chair,  go SOUTH and  then 
EAST. OPEN FRIDGE and GET ALL. you can either drop the funny bones 
or eat them,  because they aren't important in this  game...  Now, 
OPEN CARTON and OPEN MICROWAVE after this is done,  PUT CARTON  IN 
MICROWAVE  and CLOSE MICROWAVE now,  you have to set the  time..So 
TYPE 4 then TYPE 0 and then G. after that, PUSH START and WAIT (Z) 
until the microwave has reached 0:00.  Now, OPEN MICROWAVE and GET 
CARTON. go EAST and then NORTH and TRADE CHINESE FOR KEY then type 
any key like GREEN ALUMINUM and then,  GIVE CHINESE TO HACKER. now 
that you have the key,  go SOUTH and PUSH UP.  Now WAIT until  the 
elevator doors slide open.  go SOUTH and OPEN PANEL. GET LIGHT and 
PUSH OPEN.  Now, go NORTH and the DOWN twice. Go EAST and GET ALL. 
now,  we  need  protection,  so WEAR GLOVES  and  DROP  ASSIGNMENT 
(Actually,  you  can drop the assignment anywhere..you don't  need 
it) now go WEST 3 times until you are on some stairs. Go DOWN then 
NE  and  UNLOCK PADLOCK WITH KEY.   The lock  will  open,  so  GET 
PADLOCK  and OPEN HATCH.   We don't go down yet,  so go SE and  UP 
twice.   Now,  in the lobby,  go SOUTH and GET CONTAINER  now,  go 
EAST. if the waxer blocks you, just keep waiting (Z) until he move 
away and goes east. keep going east until you come to the cabinet, 
then BREAK GLASS and GET AXE now,  go WEST and OPEN CONTAINER. The 
Frobozzco  wax  and desert topping is now ready..POUR  LIQUID  and 
DROP CONTAINER.  This is a good place to save the game..  Now, CUT 
CORD WITH AXE and then WAIT until the janitor "breaks up" now,  go 
UP and CLIMB ROPE..  What the hell was that thing?!? Well, anyway, 
LOWER LADDER and then OPEN DOOR.  Out you go!  go OUT and then UP. 
GET PLUG AND DROP IT.  Wait! a paper! GET PAPER and go DOWN and IN 
and CLOSE DOOR. Jesus it's cold out there! Well, READ PAPER. Hmm.. 
I wonder who that was..  Well, go DOWN twice then EAST twice. now, 
DROP PAPER and go NORTH and then DOWN. Another Basement? Go SE and 
then GET AND WEAR BOOTS.  Now that you've got more protection,  go 
UP twice and UNLOCK DOOR WITH KEY, then OPEN DOOR. (Note: Any time 
you feel tired,  just DRINK COKE and you'll be fine) Save the game 
here, because you have to do this right. Now, go WEST and then UP. 
ah!  a  peach tree!  What the hell is that  thing  outside?  Well, 
SEARCH DIRT and GET HAND.  God,  Who's hand was this?!?  Well,  go 
DOWN and then WAIT.  It's almost on you!  THROW STONE AT THING and 
then go EAST and CLOSE DOOR.  Whew!  Close call!  now, go DOWN and 
then SOUTH. GET STONE and go NORTH,DOWN,NW,UP,SOUTH, and then DROP 
STONE.   Now,  go  WEST four times and  then  NORTH,DOWN,EAST.   A 
forklift! GET ON LIFT and then START IT. Go EAST 3 times and.. Ah! 
It's  dark!  TURN ON LIGHT and then MOVE JUNK.  you'll have to  do 
this several times,  so type G 3 times and then EAST.  Yuk! what a 
room!  Well,  GET OFF LIFT and then OPEN MANHOLE WITH  BAR.  Where 
does it lead? Go DOWN and then NORTH and DOWN. GET KNIFE.  My god, 
all  the missing students have been sacrificed!  Well,  go UP  and 
SOUTH and UP again.  now,  go WEST 5 times and then TURN LIGHT OFF 
and go UP,SOUTH and EAST 4 times.  Now,  GET PAPER.  go SOUTH  and 
KNOCK ON DOOR. Wait until the professor opens it, then SHOW PAPER. 
He  will try to pass this off,  so go SOUTH.   The professor  will 
start doing things..  Drawing Pentagrams..  What's going on? Well, 
the following commands you MUST follow,  and mistake, and you will 
die. so, WAIT (Z) twice and then CUT CHALK WITH KNIFE now, GET OUT 
OF PENTAGRAM and MOVE BENCH.  The room is darkening.. Something is 
coming..  Quickly! OPEN DOOR and then DOWN.  You'll hear screaming 
and the door will slam shut.   Well,  TURN ON LIGHT and OPEN door. 
Then  go  UP.   It  seems the professor has been  smeared  on  the 
walls..  well,  nevermind that, just GET RING and then PUT HAND IN 
VAT.   WAIT until the hand trys to crawl out of the vat,  then GET 
HAND and PUT RING ON HAND.  Go DOWN,NORTH,UP. now, keep going west 
and  look  for  the  urchin.   He  could  be  anywhere  under  the 
buildings,  or in the computer center or the aero lobby. Now, when 
you find him,  SHOW URCHIN HAND and then GET CUTTERS.   Now, go to 
the Tomb room,  where we opened the lock (You made a  map,  DIDN'T 
YOU?)  You can't get into the tomb carrying the axe,  so DROP  AXE 
and then DROP CUTTERS then enter the tomb room.  go DOWN and  then 
EAST.  and TURN VALVE WITH BAR.  Now,  WAIT until you can see  the 
rats,  then TURN VALVE WITH BAR.   Ah!  a dead rat! Well, GET RAT. 
EXAMINE RAT EXAMINE SYMBOL and DROP RAT.  Go EAST until you  can't 
go east anymore.   Now,  PRY BRICKS WITH BAR and then do it  again 
with the G command.  now,  go WEST until you can't and TURN VALVE. 
go WEST,  UP,SE,  and GET ALL.  now,  you're ready to confront the 
mother creature...   GO UP and EAST twice and UP then, PUSH UP and 
WAIT until the elevator comes.  go DOWN and OPEN DOORS then  WEDGE 
DOORS WITH BAR.  now,  go DOWN and GET CHAIN. TIE CHAIN TO ROD and 
then LOCK CHAIN TO ROD.  Go UP and PUT CHAIN ON HOOK. now, GET BAR 
and go UP twice and PUSH UP.  go DOWN twice and go EAST. go UP and 
GET FLASK.  Now,  you're ready to do battle! go DOWN,WEST and OPEN 
DOORS  then WEDGE DOORS WITH BAR.  Go DOWN and then  NORTH.   Now, 
this  is  all  the Stuff you need to  take  from  here  on:   Axe, 
Cutters,  Hand (wearing ring),  Flask,  Master Key,  Coke  Bottle, 
Flashlight,  Gloves and Boots (Wearing them), And the Stone.  Drop 
anything else.   Go WEST 6 times and go DOWN.  Now, Wait until the 
Urchins emerge and CUT WIRES WITH CUTTER and DROP CUTTER.  Go DOWN 
and then NORTH,  DOWN,  SOUTH,  SOUTH,  DOWN.   Follow the  hand's 
directions.  Now,  OPEN FLASK and POUR LIQUID ON SLIME.  Now, DROP 
FLASK and UNLOCK DOOR WITH KEY and then, OPEN DOOR.  Now, onto the 
final Confrontation!

  Go SOUTH.   Wham!  the door closes!  Well, the hand is trying to 
tell you something,  so SEARCH WATER and GET POWER.  What? Where'd 
the hacker come from??? Well, OPEN BOX and then CUT POWER WITH AXE 
now, The hacker will be pulled into the mass, so use the G command 
twice and then GET POWER.  you are now holding a live power  cord. 
now, GET COAXIAL and the hacker should be coming towards you. Now, 
PUT POWER IN SOCKET.   Zzap! The Mass will be fried and the Hacker 
will  fall.   But what is left?  Something that's Pretty  Pissed.. 
Well,  the  stone is now glowing,  but WAIT.   The thing will  get 
ready to jump,  so THROW STONE AT THING.   The thing is destroyed! 
But wait,  what happened to the stone?  GET STONE. So, you've only 
stopped the evil, but not Destroyed.. The Rock was an Egg..and the 
evil has been released, to start anew. (Good grounds for a Sequel)

 Well,  that's the end of Lurking Horror.  Look for more Soultions 
to games, Including StationFall and others, coming soon!
