   Ok, this is just another basic simple little program I decided to release
  just to help all you carders out there. This is just a utility that will
  allow you to enter a credit card number and it will search it against the
  Visa/Mastercard/Amex formulas and tell you which card it is and if it is a
  valid number. Basically that is all it does so don't expect anything superly
  spectacular.

  affiliates on it. We are looking for some great members and people who will
  be worthy to the YAM name. If you are looking to run a Distribution site then
  just talk to any YAM member.
